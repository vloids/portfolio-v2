Biography and CV

My childhood was a vibrant tapestry of diverse experiences, as I was born in Spain and moved to Belgium at a young age. These formative years instilled in me a deep appreciation for different perspectives and a passion for discovering new horizons.

In 2018, I proudly graduated from De Dames in Antwerp, armed with a diploma in Economics and Modern Languages. My thirst for knowledge led me to the esteemed KU Leuven in 2019, where I delved into the intricacies of law.

My journey took an exciting turn when I discovered the world of programming. Intrigued by its power to transform ideas into tangible realities, I enrolled in the Programming program at Thomas More Antwerp. This newfound passion ignited a spark within me, propelling me towards a future filled with innovation and technological advancements.

Throughout my life, my multicultural upbringing has been a constant source of inspiration. Surrounded by a diverse array of traditions, customs, and perspectives, I developed a deep understanding of the world's rich tapestry. This cultural fluency has shaped my approach to problem-solving, enabling me to view challenges from multiple angles and find creative solutions.

As I embark on my journey as a programmer, I am eager to harness the power of technology to connect people, bridge cultures, and make a positive impact on the world. My passion for programming is fueled by a desire to create meaningful experiences that enrich lives and empower individuals.
